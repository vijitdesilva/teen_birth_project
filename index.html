So what's the big deal?

Teen pregnancy is connected to a number of other issues that matter to communities:

  • Poverty
  • Dropout rates and low graduation rates
  • Increased reliance on social services
  • Lower attainment of higher education
  • Reduced military/deployment readiness
  • Reduced lifelong earning potential
  • Child abuse and domestic violence
